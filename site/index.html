Why forge

One harness. Any model. Your machine.

Model-agnostic

Local models via Ollama or llama.cpp, or frontier models (OpenAI, Anthropic) with your own key. Same agent, same tools — swap the weights freely.

Harness-lift

Memory, verification, escalation, and constrained output make a small model genuinely useful. forge measures the base‑vs‑harness gain, so it's evidence, not a claim.

Local & private

Runs on your machine. Your files never leave it. No account, no server, works offline — and it's stdlib‑only, zero runtime dependencies.

In three steps

From nothing to an agent.

Install

curl -fsSL …/install.sh | sh

Finds Python, installs the CLI, checks your setup.

Pick a model

forge models use phi-2

Pulled, configured, smoke-tested — ready to run.

Run

forge run "fix the failing test"

The agent works in your directory, verified end to end.
